OVERVIEW:

Getting up close with the biggest extant fish in the world is truly an experience. Therefore, oslob whale shark watching should be one of the bucket list of a traveler or adventurer. These whale sharks situate in Barangay Tan-awan, Oslob, Cebu. Further, this whale shark watching began in September 2011. However, it had surged to popularity when the news about these whale sharks reached worldwide in November 2011. So, by December of the same year, fishermen in Oslob started feeding these gentle giants. As of result of that, national and tourists would come to Oslob for this special encounter. Hence, whether you feed them or snorkel and swim around them, Oslob whale shark encounter is something you should do! You will certainly be amazed.
