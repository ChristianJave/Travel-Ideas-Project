OVERVIEW:

If you crave for thrill and for underwater experience, Kawasan canyoneering and Pescador island hopping adventures are for you. This day tour package begins with a three hour drive from Cebu City to Badian. It is in Badian where the Kawasan canyoneering happens. Kawasan canyoneering includes trekking, climbing and jumping off Kawasan Falls. A risk taker would surely be appalled by Kawasan Canyoneering experience. In the afternoon, you get to go to Moalboal for the Pescador Island hopping. The Pescador island hopping makes you explore the beauty of the underwater world. You get to see the breathtaking fishes and corals of various colors. Further, in the Pescador Island hopping, you get to swim with sardines and turtles. Kawasan canyoneering and Pescador Island hopping are a mix of adrenaline and serotonin.
